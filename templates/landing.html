About UPI / Bank Auto-Sync

SmartSpend is a manual tracking app — you enter your own transactions. Direct auto-import from UPI apps (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm) or bank accounts requires RBI-licensed Account Aggregator access, which is only available to regulated fintech companies.

Workaround: Download your bank statement as CSV/PDF and manually add the transactions — or use the quick-add button on the dashboard to log expenses right after you pay.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to common questions.

Is SmartSpend AI completely free?
Yes — 100% free, always. No plans, no credit card, no hidden limits. Every feature is available to every user.
Will my dashboard show data from previous accounts?
No. Every new account starts empty. Your dashboard shows only what you personally enter. There is no pre-loaded demo data.
Can it auto-import from PhonePe / GPay / bank?
Not automatically. Direct UPI/bank sync requires RBI-licensed infrastructure not available to open-source apps. You can manually enter transactions using the quick-add button, or import from your bank's CSV statement.
How does the AI analysis work?
SmartSpend analyses your income vs spending each month, calculates a health score out of 100, flags overspending categories, and suggests improvements based on the 50/30/20 rule and your actual patterns.
Where is my data stored?
In a local SQLite database on the machine running the app. Nothing is sent to external servers. Your finances stay completely private.
Can I switch between dark and light mode?
Yes — click the half-circle icon in the top-right corner of any page. Your preference is saved instantly to your account so it persists across sessions.
